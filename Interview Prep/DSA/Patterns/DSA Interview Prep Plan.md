## 🧪 Practice Protocol (For Every Problem)

> [!important] Follow this for **every single problem**:
> 1. **Say brute force first** — what's the naive approach?
> 2. **Identify the bottleneck** — where's the wasted work?
> 3. **Identify the pattern** — which of the 10 patterns applies?
> 4. **Explain the optimization** — why is this pattern faster?
> 5. **Code it** — clean, readable, with edge cases
> 6. **Trace through** — walk through a small example
