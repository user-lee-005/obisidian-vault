## 💡 How Strong Candidates Actually Solve Problems

They do **NOT** immediately code. They:

1. **Identify pattern**
2. **Discuss brute force**
3. **Optimize**
4. **Explain tradeoffs**
5. **Code cleanly**
6. **Test edge cases**

> [!tip] For EVERY problem, practice this flow:
> 1. Say brute force first
> 2. Identify the bottleneck
> 3. Identify the pattern
> 4. Explain the optimization
>
> *Example:* "Brute force recalculates overlapping windows repeatedly, so sliding window reduces redundant computation." — That sounds experienced.
